MEET OUR FAHD UGV

Designed for reconnaissance, surveillance and target acquisition (RSTA) missions to increase the security of personnel manning forward locations. It can be positioned in remote areas unabled to monitor by personnel.

Operation of the platform is easy, each wheel has its own electric engine with epitrochoid harmonic drive to ensure robust torque transmission to ensure steady footing in rough terrain and on steep slopes. Remote control is enabled via secure radio control channel with backup fiber-optic line. Each IRONCLAD radio system extends reach of network coverage and allows connection of additional MESH nodes. Modular layered armor allows quick replacement of damaged parts.
